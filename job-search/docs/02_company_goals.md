# 02 — Company Goals & Hiring Focus by Sector

## Core Target Companies

### Banking & Finance

| Company | HQ | Sector Focus | Cloud Strategy | DevOps Hiring Focus | Key Locations |
|---------|-----|-------------|----------------|---------------------|---------------|
| **Société Générale** | Paris | Investment banking, retail banking | Azure-first, hybrid cloud | Platform teams, SRE, DevSecOps compliance | Paris (La Défense), Casablanca (nearshore) |
| **BNP Paribas** | Paris | Universal banking, asset management | Multi-cloud (AWS + Azure) | DevOps at scale, cloud migration, CI/CD industrialization | Paris, Lisbon, Brussels, Casablanca |
| **BPCE / Natixis** | Paris | Cooperative banking, insurance | Azure + on-prem migration | Cloud engineers, IaC specialists | Paris, Nantes |

**Sector insight — Banking**: Heavy compliance (PCI-DSS, DORA regulation). Focus on **DevSecOps**, secret management, immutable infrastructure. Long hiring cycles (4–8 weeks). CDI preferred. Freelance via ESN (SSII).

## Strategic Summary

| Sector | Best for role | Hiring speed | Freelance friendly | Remote friendly |
|--------|--------------|-------------|-------------------|----------------|
| Banking | DevSecOps, Platform | Slow (4–8 wk) | Medium (via ESN) | Hybrid mostly |
| Insurance | Cloud, MLOps | Medium (3–5 wk) | Medium | Hybrid |
| Telecom | SRE, DevOps | Fast (2–4 wk) | High | Yes |
| Retail | DevOps, Platform | Fast (2–3 wk) | High | Yes |
| ESN/Consulting | All roles | Fast (1–3 wk) | Low (CDI focus) | Varies |

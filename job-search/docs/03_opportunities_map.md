## Search Keywords to Use

### English queries
```
"devops engineer" AND (AWS OR Azure) AND Kubernetes
"cloud engineer" AND Terraform AND (France OR Netherlands OR Morocco)
"platform engineer" AND Kubernetes
"devsecops" AND (banking OR insurance)
"SRE" AND (observability OR reliability)
"mlops engineer" AND (AI OR machine learning)
```

### French queries
```
"ingénieur devops" AND (AWS OR Azure)
"ingénieur cloud" AND Kubernetes AND Terraform
"ingénieur plateforme" cloud
"devsecops" sécurité CI/CD
"SRE" fiabilité observabilité
```

## Phase 3: Close & Negotiate (Week 7–12)

### Goals
- Convert interviews into offers
- Compare and negotiate
- Accept best offer

### Actions

- [ ] Prepare for technical interviews: Kubernetes scenarios, Terraform live coding, CI/CD design questions
- [ ] Prepare STAR stories for behavioral interviews (French + English)
- [ ] Research salary bands using Glassdoor, Levels.fyi, APEC salary tool
- [ ] For freelance: prepare TJM negotiation (benchmark: 450–650€/day)
- [ ] For CDI: prepare total comp negotiation (base + bonus + remote + certifications)
- [ ] Reduce application volume, focus on active pipelines
- [ ] Continue follow-ups on warm leads
- [ ] Attend 1–2 final interviews per week

### KPIs — End of Week 12
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Total applications | 80–100 |
| Interviews completed | 10–15 |
| Offers received | 2–3 |
| Offer accepted | 1 |

## Emergency Pivot (if no traction by Week 4)

If you have < 3 responses by Week 4:

1. **CV audit**: Ask 2 people in DevOps to review your CV
2. **Keyword mismatch?**: Compare your CV keywords against job descriptions
3. **Expand scope**: Add more ESN companies (Accenture, Devoteam, Inetum)
4. **Lower bar**: Apply to roles 1 level below target to get interviews flowing
5. **Freelance shortcut**: Register on Malt + Free-Work with aggressive availability
6. **Get referrals**: Ask every contact: "Do you know anyone hiring for DevOps?"
